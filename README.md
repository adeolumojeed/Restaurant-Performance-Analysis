# Restaurant-Performance-Analysis
Restaurant Performance Analysis is an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes parking availability, customer reservations, ratings, revenue, and ROI. The project provides actionable insights into restaurant performance, helping stakeholders optimize operations, improve customer satisfaction, and make data-driven business decisions.

# 

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes restaurant performance using operational and financial data. The dashboard explores the relationship between parking availability, customer reservations, ratings, revenue, and Return on Investment (ROI) to help restaurant managers make data-driven decisions that improve operational efficiency and profitability.

## Business Problem

Restaurants need to understand the factors that influence revenue and customer satisfaction. This project evaluates how parking availability, reservation trends, customer ratings, and ROI impact overall business performance.

## Project Objectives

* Analyze the impact of parking availability on revenue.
* Compare weekend and weekday reservation trends.
* Examine the relationship between customer ratings and revenue.
* Evaluate ROI across restaurant operations.
* Identify key drivers of restaurant performance.

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

* Executive KPI Cards
* Revenue by Parking Availability
* Weekend vs. Weekday Reservations
* Revenue by Customer Rating
* Rating vs. Revenue Scatter Plot
* ROI Performance Gauge
* Parking Distribution Analysis
* Revenue vs. ROI Analysis
* Interactive Slicers and Drill-through

## Key Insights

* Restaurants with parking facilities generally generated higher revenue.
* Weekend reservations exceeded weekday reservations, indicating peak customer demand.
* Higher customer ratings were associated with increased revenue and stronger ROI.
* Restaurants with strong ROI consistently delivered better financial performance.
* Customer reservations and satisfaction were key indicators of overall business success.

## Recommendations

* Improve parking accessibility where feasible to enhance customer convenience.
* Allocate additional staff and inventory during peak weekend periods.
* Focus on improving customer experience to increase ratings and repeat visits.
* Monitor ROI regularly to identify opportunities for operational improvement.

## Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard demonstrates how restaurant data can be transformed into actionable business insights. By combining operational metrics with financial performance indicators, the dashboard supports informed decision-making and highlights opportunities to improve revenue, customer satisfaction, and long-term profitability.
